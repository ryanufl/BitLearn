About BitLearn!

Blockchain is a decentralized and distributed ledger technology that allows for secure and transparent recording of transactions. It serves as the underlying technology for cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin. Miners play a crucial role in the blockchain network. They are rewarded for successfully mining blocks. The first commercial transaction with Bitcoin was on May 22, 2010 by Laszlo Hanyecz, who bought two supreme pizzas from Papa John's for a whopping 10,000 BTC, or $266 million at current prices! This began the age of using Bitcoin as a substitute for cash in transactions.
